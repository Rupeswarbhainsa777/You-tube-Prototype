Step 1

First-Run Consent

On first launch, the user sees a clear, itemised consent screen. Each data purpose has its own toggle — nothing is bundled. The user chooses exactly what they're comfortable sharing.

  • Individual toggles per purpose
  • "Why we ask" explainer for each
  • Pre-checked only where necessary
  • YouTube works even if all are off
Step 3

Privacy Center

The user taps their profile avatar to access the Privacy Center. All consent choices are visible at a glance with their current status (On/Off), and data rights actions are one tap away.

  • Consent status per category
  • Self-serve data rights section
  • View, correct, download, or erase data
  • No support tickets needed

Step 2

Home Feed + Confirmation

After confirming choices, the user lands on their personalised Home feed. A green toast banner confirms their privacy choices have been saved, reinforcing transparency and control.

  • Green success toast notification
  • Personalised video recommendations
  • Profile avatar → Privacy Center
  • Seamless, uninterrupted experience
Step 4

Data Rights Confirmation

When the user exercises a data right (view, correct, download, or erase), they immediately receive a confirmation receipt with a reference number and estimated timeline — no ambiguity.

  • Instant acknowledgement
  • Unique reference number
  • Clear SLA timeline (within 7 days)
  • Easy return to Privacy Center
